History

Founded in 1998 by adventure enthusiast and river guide Mark Thornton, River Fever began as a small operation on the banks of the Colorado River. Mark's vision was to create a rafting company that combined high-energy adventure with top-tier safety and environmental responsibility. What started with just a couple of rafts and a handful of guides quickly grew into a nationally recognized name in whitewater rafting.

Today, River Fever operates in multiple states and serves thousands of thrill-seekers each year. With a legacy built on passion, excitement, and a love for the great outdoors, River Fever continues to lead the way in whitewater rafting experiences.
