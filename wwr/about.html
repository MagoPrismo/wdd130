River Fever

Looking for an adrenaline-pumping adventure on the water? Look no further than River Fever, the premier rafting company that delivers thrilling, safe, and unforgettable experiences on some of the most breathtaking rivers in the country. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned pro, River Fever is your go-to choice for the ultimate whitewater journey.

History

Founded in 1998 by adventure enthusiast and river guide Mark Thornton, River Fever began as a small operation on the banks of the Colorado River. Mark's vision was to create a rafting company that combined high-energy adventure with top-tier safety and environmental responsibility.

Today, River Fever operates in multiple states and serves thousands of thrill-seekers each year. With a legacy built on passion, excitement, and a love for the great outdoors, River Fever continues to lead the way in whitewater rafting experiences.
