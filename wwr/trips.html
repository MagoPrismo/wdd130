Some of our Trips!

Brace yourself for a wild ride through the legendary Thunder Gorge! Known for its towering cliffs and roaring Class IV-V rapids, this route is for the bold and brave. Expect steep drops, big waves, and non-stop adrenaline as you charge through one of the most intense white water runs in the region. Ideal for experienced paddlers and adventure junkies ready to push their limits.

Looking for a gentler ride with breathtaking views? Crystal River Run is perfect for families, beginners, or anyone who wants to soak up nature at a relaxed pace. With calm Class I-II waters, sparkling pools, and forested banks, this float trip is all about fun, connection, and making memories. Great for kids and first-timers!

Leave the noise behind and dive into the untouched beauty of Bearclaw Canyon. This hidden gem winds through remote terrain, offering mid-level Class III-IV rapids, rugged cliffs, and chances to spot wildlife along the way. It’s a perfect balance of thrill and tranquility, with overnight camping options for a deeper wilderness experience.

Not for the faint of heart, Devil’s Drop delivers some of the fastest, steepest, and most technical rapids you’ll ever ride. This is where you prove your mettle. With expert guides leading the charge and top-tier gear, you’ll tackle insane drops, tight turns, and crashing waves like a pro. High risk, high reward – this is peak rafting.

For something truly unique, book a sunset or twilight run through Moonlight Bend. This calm, winding river offers a magical rafting experience under the colors of the evening sky. Perfect for couples, groups, or anyone who wants to see the water in a new light. Add on a riverside dinner or bonfire for the ultimate chill vibes.
